Prioritizing sustainable growth
Thoughtful Planning. Data-driven testing. Actionable analysis.

Incendium is a growth studio and consultancy specializing in go-to-market strategy, campaign management, and strategic advisory for technology and services companies. We offer operational support, executive-level guidance, and practical insights to rapidly-growing businesses of all sizes.

We are not an outsourced sales firm. We work in partnership with clients to craft and test go-to-market strategies, prioritizing lean testing, market understanding, and strategic analysis over quick wins. With an ultimate focus on long-term ROI, we go beyond operational execution in order to understand core business problems and creatively solve them.

Our Model

Execution

Incendium tailors solutions to each client in order to add value to the growth process. Our toolkit includes expertise in:

  • Go-to-market strategy
  • Marketing copy
  • Campaign management
  • Reporting and analysis
  • Sales tactics and coaching
Learn more
Investment

Incendium leverages our expertise to invest in promising early-stage companies. We add value to our portfolio by serving as trusted growth advisors and by assisting in new market launches.
‍
Incendium also acquires small enterprises with defensible technology that we then scale using cold outbound techniques. We are able to produce outsized returns by building and leveraging sustainable growth platforms.

Who We Work With

Incendium works with technology and services companies across a wide variety of stages. Our clients include early startups with founder-led outbound efforts, growth-stage firms with dozens of salespeople, and public companies operating across a variety of inbound and outbound channels.

Our work is flexible and tailored to the needs of each client, supporting and complementing in-house capabilities or building new business development channels from scratch. We are adept at both identifying and capitalizing on early product-market fit as well as structuring and scaling massive outbound growth efforts and we have sold into virtually all industries and client types.
